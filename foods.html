"Savoring Oahu"

A Food Lover's Paradise

Burgers

These are sandwiches of ground meat patties, cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, and other toppings, served on buns.

Crepes

These are thin pancakes that can be filled with sweet or savory ingredients.

Cheesecake

A sweet dessert made of cream cheese, eggs, sugar, and a crust of graham crackers or cookies.

Akai Bowl

These are healthy and refreshing bowls of blended acai berries topped with fresh fruits, granola, honey, and other ingredients.

Ramen

A Japanese dish of noodles in a rich broth, topped with meat, eggs, vegetables, and other ingredients

French Cuisine

A style of cooking that originated in France, characterized by its use of butter, cream, herbs, wine, cheese, and other ingredients.

Huli Huli Chicken

A flavorful and often grilled or rotisserie-cooked chicken marinated in a sweet and savory sauce.

Poke

A Hawaiian dish of raw fish marinated in soy sauce, sesame oil, and other seasonings, usually served over rice or salad.

Loco Moco

A Hawaiian dish of white rice topped with a hamburger patty, a fried egg, and brown gravy.

Spam Musubi

A popular snack made with Spam (a canned meat product) placed on a block of rice and wrapped in nori (seaweed).

Shave Ice

A Hawaiian dessert of shaved ice flavored with syrups, often served with ice cream or azuki beans.

Tempura

A Japanese dish of deep-fried seafood and vegetables coated in a light batter.
